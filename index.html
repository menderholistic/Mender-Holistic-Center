Expat Anxiety & Adjustment Support

Rooted in Compassion and Science

Living abroad brings unique emotional challenges — anxiety, overthinking, loneliness, and the overwhelm of constant adjustment. You don't have to navigate this alone. I will walk with you to manage anxiety, reduce overthinking, combat loneliness, and find balance and calm. Using cognitive behavioral therapy, mindfulness practice, and science-based tools, I help expats gain relief, clarity, and emotional stability while adjusting to life in a new country.

I understand the expat experience — the late-night overthinking, the waves of anxiety, the loneliness that creeps in when you least expect it. I will walk with you through this, not as someone who has all the answers, but as a companion on the journey.
