Feel calmer, grounded, and supported—no matter where life abroad takes you.

Rooted in Compassion and Science

Life as an expat can be exciting—but it can also feel isolating, stressful, and overwhelming. You don't have to navigate this alone.

My work combines calm, non-judgmental conversation with practical tools and structured sessions to restore clarity and emotional balance. I offer a safe space to talk and be heard, as well as guided support to understand and manage stress patterns—whether you're navigating the challenges of raising children far from family, struggling with loneliness, or finding it hard to adjust to a new culture.

Together, we'll find the balance and calm that feel out of reach right now, so you can thrive while living abroad.

Who is this for?

Expat Parents Balancing It All

You're raising children abroad while managing work and family life, often without the support network of grandparents, relatives, or close friends back home. The juggle feels exhausting and isolating.

You overthink and feel disconnected

Your mind races with worries about adapting to a new culture, your family's wellbeing, or whether you made the right choice. You feel isolated even when surrounded by others, unable to shake the loneliness.

Difficult Transition or Adjustment

Moving abroad brought unexpected challenges. You're struggling to find your footing, build meaningful connections, or create a sense of home in this new place. The adjustment feels harder than you anticipated.

My Approach

I believe genuine conversation and human connection, supported by practical structured strategies, create the foundation for lasting change. My role is to walk beside you as a fellow traveler—creating a warm, judgment-free space where you can speak openly about what you're experiencing. Whether you're navigating the complexities of parenting abroad, adjusting to a new culture, or struggling with the isolation that comes from being far from home, I meet you where you are. Through consistent, compassionate dialogue, we build trust that allows you to reconnect with your own inner wisdom and strength. This isn't about fixing you; it's about rediscovering the clarity and balance that are already within you.
